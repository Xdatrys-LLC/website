Who Reads Pocket Lens

An audience that buys.

Pocket Lens readers are internationally mobile, travel-curious, and actively investing in their photography. They purchase smartphones, accessories, travel experiences, apps, and photography tools. They research before they buy, and they recommend what they discover to the people around them.

This is not a passive audience. Pocket Lens readers open the newsletter because they want to improve. They click because the content is relevant. They buy because the recommendation is trusted.

Pocket Lens currently publishes in English. The three largest smartphone photography markets in the world — China (809 million photographers), India (547 million), and Indonesia (156 million) — are non-English speaking. Brazil, Japan, Russia, Nigeria, Mexico, and Pakistan collectively add hundreds of millions more. These markets represent the next phase of Pocket Lens expansion into Mandarin, Hindi, Portuguese, and other languages. Brands that partner with Pocket Lens now establish commercial relationships ahead of that global growth.
